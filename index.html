Meet Ryan

Built on experience, honesty and pride in the job

My career in refrigeration and air conditioning began over 13 years ago when I started out as an apprentice. Since then, I have built extensive hands-on experience installing, servicing and maintaining domestic and commercial air conditioning systems.

I hold NVQ Level 2 and NVQ Level 3 qualifications in Refrigeration & Air Conditioning, and RMA HVAC is REFCOM certified. I founded the business in 2018 with one clear aim: to provide customers with honest advice, reliable communication and workmanship completed to a standard I am proud to put my name to.

Whether the project is a single-room system, conservatory, garden office or commercial installation, I treat every property with care and make sure the right system is recommended for the space — not simply the most expensive option.

Frequently asked questions

Helpful answers before you enquire

Can air conditioning heat my home?

Yes. Modern systems provide both efficient heating and cooling, making them useful throughout the year.

How often should an air conditioning system be serviced?

Most systems benefit from servicing at least once a year, although heavily used commercial systems may require more frequent visits.

Do you provide free quotations?

Yes. RMA HVAC provides free, no-obligation quotations across the East Midlands.
